# Social Media Etiquette Checklist

## Do’s
- Use a professional profile picture and clear name.
- Share content that adds value (academic, career, or skill-related).
- Respect others’ opinions and engage politely in discussions.
- Give credit when sharing someone else’s work or ideas.
- Keep posts concise, clear, and free of slang in professional contexts.
- Double-check grammar and spelling before posting.
- Maintain privacy by adjusting account settings appropriately.

## Don’ts
- Avoid posting offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
- Don’t overshare personal details (addresses, phone numbers, private emails).
- Refrain from spamming or excessive self-promotion.
- Don’t engage in arguments or negative comment threads.
- Avoid posting during class or work hours if it affects productivity.
- Never share confidential academic or company information.

---

Following these guidelines helps maintain a professional digital presence and ensures respectful, safe interactions online.
