# Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

## Safe Digital Practices
- Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords over phone calls, emails, or messages.
- Verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies.
- Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
- Regularly update apps, operating systems, and antivirus software.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading attachments from unknown sources.

## Financial Safety
- Check transaction alerts immediately and report unauthorized activity.
- Use official banking apps and websites only.
- Do not respond to random calls or messages asking for UPI verification.
- Set transaction limits to minimize potential losses.

## Social Media & Communication
- Keep personal information private and adjust privacy settings.
- Be cautious of friend requests or messages from unknown accounts.
- Avoid oversharing sensitive details like addresses or financial data.

## Reporting & Awareness
- Report fraud promptly to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or helpline 1930.
- Inform your bank immediately about suspicious activity.
- Participate in digital literacy programs to stay updated on new threats.

---

**Conclusion:**  
Staying alert, verifying sources, and practicing safe digital habits are the most effective ways to prevent cybercrime.
